The Central Computing Facility was launched by FISAT in May 2009. The CCF contains nine labs with a total of 500 computers, all of which are equipped with Intel Core i5/Core i3 processors, 4GB RAM/ 8GB RAM/ 18.5" TFT Monitors, and Debian GNU Linux or Ubuntu as operating systems. This 15,000-square-foot high-tech lab complex is entirely air-conditioned and has an uninterruptible power supply. The campus has about 1200 computers and servers, all of which are connected by a single LAN. Staff and students can access their data at any time through the intranet. Our servers are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The interiors and layout are designed in a contemporary style for maximum utility for space and aesthetics.


Campus life

The campus provides the finest ambience for the overall development of an Individual with state-of-the-art facilities for creative teaching, experiential learning, arts, sports, professional development, research and development. The teaching-learning methodology and innovative practices adopted in the campus makes the learning experience stress free, encouraging as well as rewarding.
